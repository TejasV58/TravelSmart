Overview

Golden Triangle tour package spanning over the period of 4 days is your golden ticket to explore the rich heritage, history and culture of India, a cultural razmatazz coupled with unhurried luxury.

From an array of UNESCO World Heritage SItes to some resplendent forts and palaces in Rajsathan and from the colorful bazaars of Jaipur to the shimmering Taj Mahal in Agra, this tastefully crafted golden triangle tour itinerary is your shortcut to the very soul of India.

Itinerary

DAY 1

Arrive at Madurai, report to coach & proceed to hotel. Evening visit light & sound show & Mahatma Gandhi Museum

DAY 2

Visit Meenakshi temple - Madurai's claim to fame for its meticulous colorful carvings on the Gopurams (towers), in the vivid Dravidian image of Gods, Goddess, animal and mythical figures. Later proceed to Rameshwaram.

DAY 3

Early morning take Shpatik linga Darshan at the famous Ramanathan temple - a Master Piece in Dravidian Architecture. Proceed to Kanyakumari via Tiruchendur â€“ the sea shore temple of Subrahmanyam, is one of the delightful spots sanctified and venerated by every Hindu. Arrive in Kanyakumari. Enjoy sunset in the evening.

DAY 4

Visit Swami Vivekanand Rock Memorial. Visit the meditation hall and the smarak. Visit Kanyakumari mandir (One of the Fifty Two Shakti Peethas), Tri smudra milan & Gandhi mandapam. Evening visit Suchindram temple. (One of the Fifty Two Shakti Peethas). Proceed to Trivendrum. Arrive in Trivendrum.

DAY 5

In the morning Visit Padmanabh Swami Temple, Raja Ravi Varma Art Gallery and Napier Museum (subject to operation). Proceed to Trivendrum Airport. Board the flight to your Home Town. Tour Concludes.
