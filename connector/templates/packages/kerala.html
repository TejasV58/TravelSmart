Overview

Kerala boasts of India's most breathtaking cities, pristine backwaters and bustling beaches. This tour combines all the ingredients of an amazing tour: picturesque hills, pristine beaches, heritage sites, temples, palaces, forts, pulsating night life and, of course, lip-smacking cuisine.

The Best of Kerala tour package covers destinations like Cochin, Munnar, Alleppey, Kovalam and Thiruvananthapuram offering you the best of God's own Country in a single platter.

Itinerary

DAY 1

Arrive at the Cochin airport/railway station from where you'll be genially received and transferred to the hotel.

Following a sumptuous lunch at hotel, you'll be escorted for a sightseeing trip of the city. You'll be visiting Cochin Harbor, Fort Cochin, Dutch Palace, St Francis Church and Jewish Synagogue. Spend the day at leisure exploring the surroundings. Night stay is scheduled at hotel..

DAY 2

On the Day 2, after breakfast, hit the road to Munnar. En route you'll be enthralled by the sight of tea plantations and cardamom hills.

On your arrival, check into a Munnar Hotel. The rest of the day is all yours to spend at leisure. Night stay is scheduled at Munnar hotel.

DAY 3

On the Day 3, engage yourself on end with the sightseeing of Munnar. Explore the breathtaking hill station. You'll be visiting Mattupetty Dam, Eravikulam National Park, Dairy Farm, Old Town and the market.

The night is spent at the hotel.

DAY 4

Following breakfast on the Day 5, proceed towards Alleppey or Alappuzha. The beautiful town has a network of rivers and canals and aptly called the Venice of the East. From Alleppey, you'll be boarding a houseboat - Kettuvallam - to travel afloat the serene waters of Kerala. The experience is incredible, to say the least. You'll surely enjoy to the hilt an unforgettable cruise on the backwaters.

The night is spent on the houseboat.

DAY 5

On the Day 5, continue on to the pristine Kovalam Beach. Kovalam is regarded as one of the top ten beaches of Asia. On arrival, check into a beach hotel. Explore the beach for the rest of the day.

DAY 6

The penultimate day of Best of Kerala Tour is spent exploring the city of Thiruvananthapuram. You'll be escorted to visit Padmanabha Swamy Temple, Museum Complex, Science and Technology Museum, Shangmugham Beach Aakulam Boat Club and the Veli Tourist Village.

Night stay is scheduled at Kovalam.

DAY 7

It's time to say adieu. You'll be transferred to airport to connect the flight back home.
