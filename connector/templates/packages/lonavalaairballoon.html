Overview

About Hot Air Balloon Safari, Lonavala: Take off on an unforgettable flight high in the sky with this hot air balloon safari in Lonavala. It is located in the misty Sahyadris which adds a stunning view to the experience. It is located at a close distance from both Mumbai and Pune. The hot air balloon ride happens at different time slots all through the year and lasts for an unforgettable hour.

Pickup from Common Meeting Point ( Hotel Orritel, Talegaon) followed by a short safety briefing by the pilot along with Tea/Coffee and light refreshments are served. Duration of flight will be approximately 60 minutes (+/- 15 minutes) of flight time. Post the activity, first flight Certificate handed over to the guests. Guests are free to stay for as long as they would like after the Balloon has landed, where they have an option to interact with local villagers and watch the Balloon being deflated. Customised breakfast package services can be requested at an additional cost about which the tour operator should be informed in advance .Packaged drinking water will be provided on board

The activity of Hot Air Balloon ride in Lonavala can be availed at the following timings:

October-November: 6:15 AM
December-February: 6:45 AM & 4 PM
March-May: 6:15 AM

Duration of Hot Air Balloon Safari, Lonavala: 1 hour

Meeting point: Hotel Orritel, Talegaon

Package Offers: To & fro transport starting from Meeting Point, Tea/coffee with cookies before Safari, Balloon Safari.

Child Policy: Children are not allowed in this activity.
